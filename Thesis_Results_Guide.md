## 2. Which Method is the Best?
**Advanced Spectral Gating (DeepFilter / RNNoise)** is definitively the best method. 
* **Why the others fail:** Wiener, Band-Pass, and High-Pass filters are linear. They blindly block out certain frequencies (like cutting out everything below 150Hz). This is bad for local language support (Sinhala) because it accidentally cuts out important vocal tones, causing the AI to misunderstand the words.
* **Why Spectral Gating wins:** It is non-stationary. It calculates a dynamic "noise profile" during the silent gaps between your words, and actively subtracts *only* that noise profile from the entire audio clip. It leaves the Sinhala phonemes perfectly intact while erasing the background noise.
